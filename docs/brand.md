# Brand Guidelines

## Brand architecture

| Layer | Usage |
|-------|--------|
| **ReyGris** | Primary brand — marketing, product, social, domain |
| **ReyGris Technologies** | Legal, contracts, formal correspondence |
| **Gray Kingbird** | Symbolic reference in storytelling, not a product name |

## Brand story (elevator)

ReyGris is a technology company named for the Gray Kingbird — a bird of home, vigilance, and unmistakable presence. We build software and systems with the same clarity and care: rooted where we come from, sharp where it counts.

## Voice & tone

### Voice (consistent)
- **Confident** — not arrogant
- **Direct** — short sentences, active voice
- **Warm** — human, occasionally poetic, never corporate-empty
- **Precise** — technical when needed, plain language by default

### Tone (contextual)

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Marketing / landing | Inspiring, grounded, concise |
| Documentation | Clear, instructional, neutral |
| Client communication | Professional, warm, honest |
| Internal standards | Direct, specific, no fluff |

### Words we use
- build, craft, vigilance, rooted, home, territory, standards, clarity, stewardship

### Words we avoid
- synergy, leverage (as a verb), disruptive, revolutionary, ninja, rockstar, best-in-class

## Color palette

The ReyGris palette draws from the Gray Kingbird: slate grays, soft neutrals, and a restrained gold accent suggesting *Rey* (king) without shouting royalty.

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| Primary gray | Kingbird Slate | `#5C6670` | Headings, primary UI chrome |
| Deep gray | Territory Charcoal | `#2D3748` | Body text, dark backgrounds |
| Light gray | Feather Mist | `#E8ECF0` | Backgrounds, borders |
| Off-white | Sky Wash | `#F7F9FB` | Page background |
| Accent gold | Crown Gold | `#B8956A` | Accents, highlights, links on dark backgrounds |
| Accent deep | Crown Deep | `#8B6914` | Hover states, emphasis |

## Typography

For the static site and general brand usage:

| Role | Font stack | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| Headings | `Georgia, "Times New Roman", serif` | Evokes presence and authority (*Rey*); system fonts only, no dependencies |
| Body | `-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif` | Clean, readable, dependency-free |

When producing markdown or other materials, the same logic applies: prefer system font stacks or widely available web-safe choices unless you embed custom fonts intentionally.

## Logo usage

The ReyGris mark is a stylized Gray Kingbird silhouette with a subtle crown motif — see the static site for the inline SVG implementation.

### Clear space
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least the height of the bird mark on all sides.

### Minimum size
Do not render the logomark smaller than 24px in height for digital use.

### Color variants
- **Primary:** Kingbird Slate on Sky Wash or white
- **Reversed:** White or Feather Mist mark on Territory Charcoal
- **Accent:** Crown Gold accent elements may be used on the bird/crown details; keep the mark single-color when possible for small sizes

## Imagery direction

Photography and illustration should evoke:

- Open skies and horizon lines (the bird's domain)
- Caribbean and tropical light without cliché overload
- Minimal, geometric bird forms — silhouette over literal illustration
- Grays, soft whites, and restrained gold accents

Avoid stock photos of generic corporate handshakes, abstract "tech blue" gradients, or overly literal crown imagery.
