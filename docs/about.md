# About ReyGris

## The name

**ReyGris** — Spanish for *Gray King* — honors the Gray Kingbird, a striking bird that is unmistakable across the skies of the Caribbean and much of the Americas. The full legal name is **ReyGris Technologies**, but we lead with **ReyGris** for clarity, warmth, and brand recall.

## Why this bird

The Gray Kingbird is more than a mascot. It is a symbol chosen deliberately:

| Trait | What it means for ReyGris |
|-------|---------------------------|
| **Home** | Rooted in where we come from; we build with identity, not anonymity |
| **Vigilance** | Sharp attention to detail, quality, and the people we serve |
| **Territory & protection** | We defend craft, reliability, and trust in everything we ship |
| **Distinct voice** | Like the kingbird's call, we communicate clearly and without noise |
| **Adaptability** | Thrives across environments — we solve real problems in varied contexts |
| **The crown in the name** | *Rey* signals leadership, standards, and excellence without arrogance |

## What we do

ReyGris Technologies builds thoughtful technology — software, systems, and digital experiences — with the same precision and presence the Gray Kingbird brings to its territory. We combine technical depth with human-centered design.

## What we are not

- A faceless vendor chasing trends
- A volume shop optimizing for output over outcome
- A brand that hides behind jargon

We are builders who remember where we come from and why the work matters.
