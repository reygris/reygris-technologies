# Strategic Goals

ReyGris goals are reviewed quarterly. They connect daily work to the vision and mission.

## Year-one pillars

### 1. Establish the brand
- Launch public presence (website, core messaging)
- Document standards in this repository
- Present a consistent visual and verbal identity

**Success signals:** Live site on custom domain; complete docs set; recognizable ReyGris mark

### 2. Deliver with proof
- Ship at least one flagship project or product that demonstrates craft
- Collect measurable outcomes (performance, reliability, user satisfaction)
- Build case studies that tell the ReyGris story through work, not slogans

**Success signals:** Published case study; client or user testimonial; uptime and quality metrics

### 3. Build sustainable operations
- Define engineering, communication, and quality standards (see `docs/standards/`)
- Establish repeatable delivery practices
- Document decisions and architecture for continuity

**Success signals:** Standards adopted on active projects; onboarding doc usable by a new contributor in one day

### 4. Grow intentionally
- Pursue partnerships aligned with values (craft, clarity, long-term thinking)
- Say no to misaligned work without guilt
- Invest in learning that compounds (security, accessibility, platform depth)

**Success signals:** Pipeline of qualified opportunities; zero regret projects

## Long-term horizon (3–5 years)

| Goal | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Regional recognition** | Known in our home market and beyond for reliable, thoughtful tech |
| **Product + services balance** | Own products where it makes sense; consult where impact is highest |
| **Team depth** | Small, excellent team — quality over headcount |
| **Open contribution** | Share standards, tools, or knowledge that help the broader community |

## What we optimize for

1. **Trust** — clients and users return because we deliver
2. **Clarity** — everyone knows what we stand for and how we work
3. **Durability** — systems and relationships that last years, not sprints

## What we do not optimize for

- Vanity metrics (follower counts without engagement)
- Revenue at the cost of values
- Feature count over outcome quality

## Review cadence

| Review | Frequency | Output |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Goal progress | Quarterly | Updated metrics in this doc |
| Vision/mission fit | Annually | Confirm or revise north stars |
| Standards | Ongoing | PRs to `docs/standards/` as practices evolve |
